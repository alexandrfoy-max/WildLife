A Day Camp in Chayofa

Our camp is a unique opportunity to connect with the nature and culture of Tenerife through play and exploration. We offer immersive experiences perfect for children on their own or for the whole family. And between activities, you'll be treated to authentic Canarian food!

Secrets of the Canarian Mountains & Volcanoes

What lies beneath the mountains of Tenerife? What legends do the locals tell about these sleeping giants? Why are some mountains solid while others crumble like sand?

  • A guided mountain expedition with discoveries.
  • Learn about island geology and local folklore.
  • A hands-on craft workshop to create traditional Guanche artifacts.

Legends of the Canarian Deserts

How do plants survive without water? Discover the amazing world of cacti and succulents in Tenerife's unique desert landscapes.

  • A guided desert expedition through cactus fields
  • Learn how cacti store water and survive in arid conditions
  • Discover medicinal uses of desert plants by local healers
  • A hands-on craft workshop inspired by ancient Guanche people
